## What is Obserc?

Obserc is a personal safety app for iPhone that keeps you quietly connected to the people who matter most. It works in the background — so when you are walking home late, heading out with friends, or driving somewhere unfamiliar, the people you trust always know you are okay.

Most safety tools are reactive. They wait for something to go wrong. Obserc is built the other way: it creates a live safety net around you before anything happens, so the people who care about you can act fast if it ever does.

---

## Who is it for?

Obserc is built for anyone who has ever felt the quiet anxiety of being out alone — and for the people who worry about them.

In practice that means:

- **Students** heading home late from campus or a night out
- **Young women** who want trusted contacts to know they are okay without texting every hour
- **Friends** who look out for each other on nights out
- **Families** who want a shared safety layer without the noise of a group chat
- **Drivers** who want their people to know they arrived safely

---

## How it works

**1. Build your circle**
Invite the people you trust most — friends, family, a flatmate. They become your safety network inside the app.

**2. Go about your life**
Check in when you arrive somewhere. Nudge someone if you haven't heard from them. Start a Night Out before you head out. Drive somewhere and let your circle know you arrived.

**3. If something goes wrong**
One tap sends an SOS Beacon to your entire circle with your live location. Your people know immediately. You stay focused on what matters.

---

## Safety note

> Obserc is designed to support your safety habits and keep your trusted people informed. It does not replace emergency services.
>
> **If you are in immediate danger, call your local emergency number — 911, 999, 112, or equivalent — first.**
