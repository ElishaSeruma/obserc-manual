# 06 — Night Out

[← Back to manual](../README.md)

---

## What Night Out is for

Night Out is a dedicated safety mode for going out with friends. It creates a shared session between you and your squad, gives a trusted wingman visibility of your night, and provides quick access to safety tools — all without having to think about it once the session is running.

Use Night Out whenever you are heading out somewhere unfamiliar, going out late, or want your trusted people to know you are okay throughout the night.

---

## Before a Night Out starts

### Opening Night Out

Tap **Night Out** on the Status tab or from the main navigation.

If this is your first time, a short intro screen explains how Night Out works. Tap **Get started** to continue.

`[SCREENSHOT: docs/screenshots/night-out/night-out-landing.png]`

---

### Setup flow

The setup sheet walks you through the following steps before you start your session:

**1. Select a wingman**

Your wingman is one person from your circle who gets elevated visibility of your Night Out — they can see your location, your squad's status, and receive alerts if something goes wrong.

- Tap **Wingman**
- Select one member from your active circle
- Your wingman receives a notification that you have chosen them

**2. Select squad members**

Your squad are the people coming out with you. They can be circle members or Obserc users you invite.

- Tap **Add squad members**
- Select from your circle or search by username

**3. Add an itinerary (optional)**

Add the places you plan to go — a bar, a restaurant, a venue. This helps your wingman follow your night and makes Find Squad more useful.

- Tap **Add stop**
- Search for a location or drop a pin
- Repeat for each stop

**4. Set visibility**

Choose who can see your Night Out session:

- **Circle only** — only your active circle members can see you are in a Night Out
- **Squad only** — only your squad members have full visibility
- **Private** — your wingman only

**5. Start the session**

Tap **Start Night Out** to begin. The Night Out dashboard opens.

## While a Night Out is active

### Night Out dashboard

The dashboard is your control centre during an active session. It shows:

- Your squad members and their current status
- Active itinerary stops
- Quick access to all safety tools
- Session duration

`[SCREENSHOT: docs/screenshots/night-out/night-out-dashboard.png]`

---

### Night Out tabs

The dashboard has several tabs:

- **Squad** — live status of everyone in your session
- **Map** — the Night Out map showing squad locations
- **Itinerary** — your planned stops and current position in the night
- **Tools** — quick access to safety actions

---

### I Feel Off

If something feels wrong but you are not in immediate danger, tap **I Feel Off**.

This opens a panel with options to:

- Alert your wingman quietly
- Trigger a Safety Call
- Escalate to SOS Beacon

Your wingman receives a notification that you have flagged something without a full SOS being sent.

`[SCREENSHOT: docs/screenshots/night-out/night-out-i-feel-off.png]`

---

### Nudging your squad

Tap any squad member's card and select **Nudge** to send a quick check-in request. They will receive a notification asking if they are okay.

---

### Find Squad

Find Squad shows you where your squad members are on a live map so you can locate anyone who has wandered off or meet up at the next stop.

Tap **Find Squad** from the dashboard or the tools tab.

`[SCREENSHOT: docs/screenshots/night-out/night-out-find-squad.png]`

---

### Night Out map

The Night Out map shows:

- Your squad members as pins
- Your itinerary stops
- Your current location
- Meet Me pin options

Open it by tapping the **Map tab** in the Night Out dashboard.

`[SCREENSHOT: docs/screenshots/night-out/night-out-map.png]`

---

### Safety Call

Safety Call simulates an incoming phone call, giving you a believable reason to step away from a situation.

**To start a Safety Call:**

1. Tap **Safety Call** from the tools tab or I Feel Off panel
2. Set a delay (immediately, 1 minute, 3 minutes)
3. Your phone rings as if receiving a real call

`[SCREENSHOT: docs/screenshots/night-out/night-out-safety-call.png]`

---

### Quick access to emergency services

In any emergency, tap the **emergency dial** button in the Night Out tools to open your phone's dialler with the emergency number pre-filled.

> **Always call emergency services first if you are in immediate danger. This is a direct call — not an in-app feature.**

---

### Triggering Night Out SOS

Tap **SOS** from the Night Out dashboard to trigger an SOS Beacon within your Night Out session. Your squad and wingman are alerted immediately with your live location.

See [SOS →](07-sos.md) for full details on what happens after an SOS is sent.

---

### Managing members during a session

Tap **Manage session** to:

- Add or remove squad members mid-session
- Change your wingman
- View who has seen your status

`[SCREENSHOT: docs/screenshots/night-out/night-out-manage.png]`

---

### Editing your itinerary during a session

Tap **Itinerary** in the dashboard, then **Edit** to add, remove, or reorder stops while the session is running.

`[SCREENSHOT: docs/screenshots/night-out/night-out-itinerary-editor.png]`

---

## Ending a Night Out

Tap **End Night Out** from the dashboard to close your session.

You will be asked to confirm. Once ended, the session is saved to your Activity history.

---

## After a Night Out ends

### Morning After report

After your session ends, Obserc generates a **Morning After report** — a summary of your night including:

- Session duration
- Stops visited
- Squad members and their check-in status
- Any safety events that occurred

The report is available in the Activity tab and is visible only to you.
